Movie Top Rankings

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Gladiator

This is my favourite so far, mix of drama, action, revenge, and the amazing story behind this film makes it a top pick when it comes to movie nights.

I robot

Really cool sci-fi movie with an intriguing plot to say the least, in a way it could have been an apocalyptic beginning starting from a much more advanced AI.

Les Intouchables

Based on a true story, this film is a beautiful portrayal of friendship and humanity.
